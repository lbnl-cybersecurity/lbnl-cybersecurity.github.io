Open Jobs in LBNL Cybersecurity R&D

Security/Systems Postdoctoral Scholar

Berkeley Lab Computing Sciences Research has an immediate opening for a Security/Systems Postdoctoral Researcher to perform vital research and development in the area of the use of trusted execution environments (TEEs) in high-performance computing (HPC) domains. The goal of this work is to enable high-performance scientific computing of sensitive data without significantly compromising usability or performance. The work includes software development of and experimentation with security and privacy technologies critical to facilitate data collection and sharing.
