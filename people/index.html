People

Berkeley Lab’s work on cybersecurity R&D is cross-directorate and includes scientists not only from the Scientific Data Division (SciData), but also Applied Mathematics and Computational Research (AMCR), ESnet, and NERSC in Computing Sciences, as well as LBNL’s IT Division, and Energy Technology Area, and a number of external academic and industry partners.
