People

Berkeley Lab’s work on cybersecurity R&D is cross-directorate and has included scientists from the Computing Sciences (CS) research divisions, ESnet, NERSC, IT Division, Energy Technologies Area, Applied Nuclear Physics, and a number of external academic and industry partners.  It also works with the Berkeley Lab Office of National Homeland Security (ONHS).
